About Me

I graduated from Virginia Tech with a Bachelor of Science in Psychology and Statistics. Originally from Norfolk, Virginia, I moved to Arlington and have been working as a Pricing Analyst within the busy world of government contracting for the past 3 years. At my last position, I was lucky enough to have the opportunity to teach myself VBA in order to develop a custom, flexible pricing model for my company. I recognized I had a passion for programming and designing tools and interfaces. This allowed me to improve on my ability to quickly analyze data and lead meetings. Eventually, I realized that there was enormous potential to solving problems through code. I knew I wanted to focus on realizing this potential and that the first step would be to make a career change.

I recently graduated from the Full Stack Flex Web Development coding bootcamp at George Washington University in order to pursue a future in web development.
While not coding or studying, I enjoy...

  • singing and playing guitar
  • improving on my chess game
  • freelance consulting
  • making fanedits of various movies, namely Star Wars, to fix my head-canon
